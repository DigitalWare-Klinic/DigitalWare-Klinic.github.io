Resource Profile: Device

Version:1.0
Name:ProfileImplantableDeviceKlinic
Title:Device
Status:Active as of 2021-03-03
Definition:

FHIR resource profile for Active Implantable Medical Devices (AIMD), invasive medical devices, custom medical devices, and invasive or reusable surgical medical devices.

Many of these devices are partially or completely absorbed.

Such as: pacemaker, extends, catheter, IUD, and surgical instruments.
