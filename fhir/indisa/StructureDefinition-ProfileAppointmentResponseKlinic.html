Resource Profile: Appointment Response

Defining URL:https://digitalware-klinic.github.io/fhir/indisa/StructureDefinition-ProfileAppointmentResponseKlinic
Version:1.0
Name:ProfileAppointmentResponseKlinic
Title:Appointment Response
Status:Active as of 2021-02-25
Definition:

Response regarding the scheduling of the patient's appointment according to the availability of the agenda or regarding cancellations, reviews, among other activities on each appointment.
